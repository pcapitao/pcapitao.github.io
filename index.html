Pedro Capitão

Hi! I am a PhD candidate in the Cryptology Group at CWI Amsterdam, with Lisa Kohl as my supervisor. I previously obtained my master's and bachelor's degrees in applied mathematics at IST, University of Lisbon.

My research interests include secure multi-party computation, homomorphic encryption, zero-knowledge proofs and post-quantum cryptography.
